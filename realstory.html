TOP HORROR MOVIES BASED ON TRUE STORIES
1. The Conjuring (2013): 7.5/10
The Conjuring is a 2013 American supernatural horror film. In this film The Perron family moves into a farmhouse where they experience paranormal phenomena. They consult demonologists, Ed and Lorraine Warren, to help them get rid of the evil entity haunting them.
2. The Conjuring 2 (2016): 7.2/10
The Conjuring 2 is a 2016 American supernatural horror film. It is the sequel to 2013's The Conjuring. The film follows the Warrens as they travel to England to assist the Hodgson family, who are experiencing poltergeist activity at their Enfield council house in 1977 which later became referred to as the Enfield poltergeist.
3. The Conjuring: The Devil Made Me Do It (2021): 6.3/10
The Conjuring: The Devil Made Me Do It (also known as The Conjuring 3) is a 2021 American supernatural horror film. The film serves as a sequel to The Conjuring (2013) and The Conjuring 2 (2016). In this film Arne Cheyenne Johnson stabs and murders his landlord, claiming to be under demonic possession. Ed and Lorraine Warren investigate the case and try to prove his innocence.
4. The Exorcist (1973): 8.1/10
The Exorcist is a 1973 American supernatural horror film. In this film Regan, a young girl, displays bizarre behaviour after playing with a Ouija board. Chris, her mother and an actress, consults two priests who conclude that Regan is possessed by a demonic entity.
5. The Exorcism of Emily Rose (2005): 6.7/10
The Exorcism of Emily Rose is a 2005 American supernatural horror. In this film Reverend Moore performs an exorcism on a girl believed to be possessed by demons. The prosecutor argues that the girl suffered from schizophrenia, but Moore's defence lawyer has a different opinion.
6. Annabelle (2014): 5.4/10
Annabelle is a 2014 American supernatural horror film. It is a prequel to the 2013 film. The film was inspired by a story of a doll named Annabelle told by Ed and Lorraine Warren. In this film John and Mia Form are attacked by members of a satanic cult that uses their old doll as a conduit to make their life miserable. This unleashes a string of paranormal events in the Forms' residence.
7. Annabelle: Creation (2017): 6.5/10
Annabelle: Creation is a 2017 American supernatural horror film. It is a prequel to 2014's Annabelle. In this film Judy and her babysitter are left alone in her house after her parents leave to investigate a case. However, an unexpected guest sets Annabelle free, unleashing demonic activity in the house.
8. Annabelle Comes Home (2019): 5.9/10
Annabelle Comes Home is a 2019 American supernatural horror film written and directed by Gary Dauberman, in his directorial debut, from a story by Dauberman and James Wan, who also acted as producer with Peter Safran. It serves as a sequel to 2014's Annabelle and 2017's Annabelle: Creation, and as the seventh installment in the Conjuring Universe franchise. The film stars Mckenna Grace, Madison Iseman, and Katie Sarife, along with Vera Farmiga and Patrick Wilson, who reprise their roles as Ed and Lorraine Warren.
9. The Texas Chain Saw Massacre (1974): 7.4/10
The Texas Chain Saw Massacre is a 1974 American horror film. In this film Sally, Franklin and their three friends run out of gas while driving to their grandfather's house. One by one they are tormented and killed by a chainsaw-wielding killer and his psychopathic family.
10. A Nightmare on Elm Street (1984): 7.4/10
A Nightmare on Elm Street is a 1984 American supernatural slasher film. In this film a young girl realises that she has to stay awake at any cost to avoid the clawed killer who is butchering her friends one by one in their dreams. The film was followed by A Nightmare on Elm Street 2: Freddy's Revenge.
11. Child's Play (1988): 6.6/10
Child's Play is a 1988 American supernatural horror film. In this film Karen buys her son Andy a sought-after doll for his birthday and Andy names it Chucky. Things take a turn for the worse when Andy discovers that Chucky is possessed by the spirit of a serial killer. A fact, Chucky, the killer doll from the Child's Play, isn't a real life individual, he is actually inspired by an alleged true story.
12. The Possession (2012): 5.9/10
The Possession is a 2012 American supernatural horror film. In this film During a yard sale, Emily, a young girl, comes across an antique box and asks her father to buy it for her. Soon, she becomes increasingly obsessed with it and exhibits signs of being possessed.
13. The Rite (2011): 6/10
The Rite is a 2011 supernatural horror film. Like stories on exorcism and the devil? Based on the life of one of the most famous exorcists of America, Father Gary Thomas, this movie will scare the hell out of you. One piece of advice: Don't try this alone.
14. The Amityville Horror (2005): 5.9/10
The Amityville Horror is a 2005 American supernatural horror film. In this film Married couple George and Kathy move into a new home along with their children. Their happiness is short-lived once bizarre and unexplainable events begin to occur there.
15. The Haunting In Connecticut (2009): 5.8/10
The Haunting In Connecticut is a 2009 American supernatural horror film. In this film In order to help their son recover, the Campbell family moves to upstate Connecticut. But they soon discover that their new Victorian abode has a disturbing history and is haunted.
